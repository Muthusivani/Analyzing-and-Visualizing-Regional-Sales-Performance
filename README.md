# Analyzing-and-Visualizing-Regional-Sales-Performance

📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive sales dashboard designed to analyze and visualize regional sales performance across multiple product categories. It helps uncover insights into sales trends, profitability, and regional distribution.
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key metrics such as total sales, profit comparisons, product counts, and regional contributions.

Features
📈 Sales Analysis by Region
Compare sales performance across East, West, North, and South regions.
🛍️ Product-Level Insights
Track performance for categories like Clothing, Electronics, Furniture, Groceries, and Sports.

💰 Profit Monitoring
Identify minimum profit-generating products.
🔢 Product Count Metrics
Understand product distribution across categories.

📊 Dynamic Visualizations
Bar charts, pie charts, and area charts for better decision-making.
🎯 Interactive Filters
Region-based filtering for customized analysis.

🧩 Dashboard Components
Maximum Product Sales
Minimum Profit Analysis
Count of Products
Total Sales by Region & Product
Regional Distribution (Pie Chart)
Sales Amount Comparison
Overall Total Sales

📊 Key Insights

Electronics and Clothing are among the top-performing categories.
Some categories show low profitability despite high sales, indicating optimization opportunities.
Regional differences highlight potential for targeted marketing strategies.
